About Us

Our Story

From a small vision in Gurgaon to one of Haryana's most trusted solar companies — our journey has been powered by purpose.

Shree Vinayak Solar & Construction was founded with a single mission: make clean, affordable solar energy accessible to every home and business. Over the past decade, we have transformed rooftops across Gurgaon, Faridabad, and beyond — reducing carbon footprints and electricity bills simultaneously.

Our team of certified engineers and construction experts handles every project end-to-end, from site assessment and system design to installation, government subsidy assistance, and lifetime maintenance support.
